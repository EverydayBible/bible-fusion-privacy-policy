Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Bible Fusion stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Reading Preferences: Your selected app language, Bible language, theme, font size, and parallel reading mode
  • Reading History: Your last read book, chapter, and scroll position
  • Bookmarks: Verses you have bookmarked for quick access
  • Highlights: Verses you have highlighted with colors
  • Notes: Personal notes you have written about specific verses
  • Search History: Recent search queries (stored locally only)
  • Notification Settings: Your preferred notification frequency and time
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have completed the initial setup
  • Ad Watch Timestamps: When you last watched rewarded ads for premium themes
Important: All of this data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and SQLite database. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
