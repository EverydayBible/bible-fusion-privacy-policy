Privacy Policy for Telugu + English Bible Fusion

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

Introduction

Telugu + English Bible Fusion ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Quick Summary

What we collect:

  • Nothing directly - all your data stays on your device
  • AdMob collects anonymous ad data
  • Google Play processes donations

What we don't do:

  • We don't access your personal data
  • We don't sell your information
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't require account creation

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Telugu + English Bible Fusion stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Reading Preferences: Your selected app language, Bible language, theme, and font size
  • Reading History: Your last read book, chapter, and scroll position
  • Bookmarks: Verses you have bookmarked for quick access
  • Highlights: Verses you have highlighted with colors
  • Notes: Personal notes you have written about specific verses
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have completed the initial setup
Important: All of this data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and SQLite database. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Telugu + English Bible Fusion displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Advertising ID

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play In-App Purchases

When you make a donation through Google Play In-App Purchases, Google processes the transaction. We receive:

  • Purchase confirmation (to unlock premium features)
  • No payment information (handled entirely by Google Play)

For more information, please review Google Play's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

Local Data Usage

Your locally stored data is used to:

  • Provide personalized reading experience
  • Remember your preferences and reading position
  • Display your bookmarks, highlights, and notes
  • Improve app functionality

Third-Party Data Usage

  • AdMob: To display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Google Play: To process donations and verify purchases

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Play: For processing in-app purchases
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Children's Privacy

Telugu + English Bible Fusion is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data is stored locally on the device, and we do not have access to user data.

Data Security

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers
  • You can delete all app data by clearing app data in your device settings

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app
  • Delete Your Data: Uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: Adjust your device's ad settings
  • Control Permissions: Manage app permissions in your device settings

How to Delete Your Data

To completely remove all your data:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps → Bible Fusion
  3. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
  4. Or simply uninstall the app
